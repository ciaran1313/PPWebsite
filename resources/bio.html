Pterodactyl Problems is a heavy alt-rock band from Toronto, Canada initially formed in 2012, and recently reunited in 2017. Each life-long musicians with diverse backgrounds and influences spanning Opera, Jazz, Rock, Musical Theatre, Punk, Folk, Classical and Heavy Metal, the band was formed almost by accident through a unified passion for music. Pterodactyl Problems is made possible through a mix of deep synergies and intense creative conflict that their music is born by. They combine singer-songwriter compositions and vocals with heavy guitar and drums that result in a truly unique rock sound.
